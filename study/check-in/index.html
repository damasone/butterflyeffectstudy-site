Participant portal

Secure daily follow-up for your Butterfly Effect study.

Sign in once, then use the same account each day. This portal is built for repeated longitudinal follow-up, not for one-off interpretation. Your entries stay in the secure study backend and are later linked to your wearable history.

What you do here Record one short daily check-in and only log a change event when something meaningful starts, stops, or shifts.
What happens later Once enough days accumulate, the study can generate a participant review and a professional companion from the combined wearable and self-report data.
