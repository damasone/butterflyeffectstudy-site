How this study works

Daily effort One short check-in, ideally at the start of the day or soon after waking.
Data return When enough days accumulate, the study can produce a participant review and a professional companion.
Boundary This page records follow-up data. It does not diagnose insomnia, anxiety, depression, pain conditions, or any other disorder.
