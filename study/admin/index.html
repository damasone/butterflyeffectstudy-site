Study portal

Daily check-ins with a persistent participant identity.

This browser-based portal is the lightest usable version of the Butterfly Effect study workflow. It stores the participant profile and repeated daily check-ins in this browser, then exports them in a format ready for later merge with Apple Health or equivalent wearable history.

Operating model

What this portal does and does not do

What it does Keeps a persistent participant ID, preserves local history, and exports a study-ready CSV or full JSON backup.
What it does not do It does not upload data to a server, create an online account, or replace the later merge with Apple Health history.
Why it matters This is enough for a serious pilot without taking on backend security and account infrastructure too early.
